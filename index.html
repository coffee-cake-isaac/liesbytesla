Hardware

2014 - Oct 2016: HW1 (Hardware 1): This was hardware designed and developed by MobilEye called the MobilEye EyeQ3. This was released in 2014 and was labeled AP (Autopilot) HW1. This was came equipped with a forward radar, a forward camera, and 12 surrounding sonars.
Oct 2016 - August 2017: HW2 (Hardware 2): This was designed and developed by NVIDIA called NVIDIA DRIVE PX 2 AI computing platform. This came equipped with a forward radar, a front / side camera color filter array, 3 forward cameras (Natrrow, Main and Wide), two forward looking side cameras, 2 rearward looking side cameras, and 12 surrounding sonars.
August 2017 - April 2019: HW2.5 (Hardware 2.5): This was designed and developed by NVIDIA called NVIDIA DRIVE PX 2 with secondary node enabled. This came with equipped with a forward radar, a front / side camera color filter array, 3 forward cameras (Natrrow, Main and Wide), two forward looking side cameras, 2 rearward looking side cameras, and 12 surrounding sonars.
April 2019 - Current: HW3 (Hardware 3): This was designed and developed by Tesla containing two identical chips (also designed/developed by Tesla) and the rest of the hardware suite in HW2.5
